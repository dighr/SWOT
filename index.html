Safe Water Optimization Tool ---
Humanitarian agencies prioritize water safety and routinely collect, monitor and report residual chlorination data. Little is done, however, to ensure that water quality complies with guidelines at the point of consumption, a significant oversight in response efforts.

The Safe Water Optimization Tool fills that gap. Explore this site to learn more.
